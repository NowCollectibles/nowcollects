How to Source Pickups

  • 📱 Follow breaking sports news and injury reports.
  • 📈 Watch for trends: hot streaks, call-ups, or trade rumors.
  • 🛒 Search eBay right after big news hits—look for underpriced BINs.
  • 🔍 Use filters: sort by “Newly Listed,” check sold comps quickly.
  • 💬 Cross-check social buzz on Twitter/X and Discord groups.
  • ⏱ Act fast—deals disappear in minutes after headlines drop.
