🗓️ June 19, 2025 – Juan Soto Records 1,000th Career Hit as a Met

📰 Sports Card News: Highlights & Analysis

  • 🎯 Soto singled off Spencer Strider in the 1st inning to notch his 1,000th career hit at just 26 years old — one of only 84 players to reach that milestone before turning 27.
  • He’s also the only player ever under 27 with at least 1,000 hits, 200 home runs, and 800 walks.
  • Through June, Soto has bounced back from a slow start—currently slashing around .248/.387/.842 with 14 HR, 37 RBI, and 8 SB in 268 ABs.
  • June has been hot: in a recent span—.310/.481/.621 with 5 HR, 10 RBI, 16 runs through 17 games—igniting the Mets’ NL East push.
  • Despite Soto’s milestone, the Mets dropped the game 7–1 to Atlanta, extending their rough patch.

📈 What’s Next?

  • Soto is on pace for more historic milestones—around 250 HR and 1,000 career walks seem possible by next season.
  • The Mets look to regroup as they head into their next series. Soto’s offensive resurgence could be key in stabilizing their lineup.
